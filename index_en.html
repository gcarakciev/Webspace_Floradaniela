Floradaniela is a Salzburg-based company specializing in the distribution of Salzburg souvenirs, Christmas decorations, and spice arrangements (spice bouquet, spice wreath). About Us:

Welcome to Floradaniela!

Since March 1st, 2020, we have been a proud company dedicated to the trade of spice bundles and other lovingly crafted products. We are a small family-owned business based in Salzburg and founded by Mrs. Daniela Carakcieva.

Our story began with Mrs. Carakcieva's passion for spice bundles. As a native of North Macedonia, she was fascinated by the craftsmanship and beauty of these traditional wreaths and bouquets. This enthusiasm motivated her to start her own business and make these unique products accessible to others.

Since then, Mrs. Carakcieva has been leading Floradaniela with dedication and pride. Our products are of high quality and carefully handcrafted. We place great emphasis on using top-quality spices and materials to ensure that our spice bundles are not only visually appealing but also emit a delightful fragrance.

However, our range is not limited to spice bundles. We also offer a variety of souvenirs from Salzburg, Christmas decorations, and floral decorations. Our products reflect the unique beauty and charm of this city.

We take pride in offering our customers high-quality products crafted with attention to detail. Our mission is to enhance your living spaces and bring a piece of Salzburg to your home.

Thank you for choosing Floradaniela. We look forward to presenting our wonderful products to you and helping you create unforgettable memories.
